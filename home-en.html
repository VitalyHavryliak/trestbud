About us

   EKOTRESTBUD successively operates in the architectural and construction business and is a reliable partner for all types of construction work.

 

   The main activity is the restoration and renovation of old buildings (substruction reinforcement, waterproofing, ceiling and roof replacement).

 

   We also provide services in the design of all types of residential and non-residential premises, we carry out their construction with state-of-the-art technology using high quality environmentally friendly materials (all types of wood).

 

   All projects are carried out by highly skilled specialists (fitters, bricklayers, carpenters, plumbers, electricians, plasterers, painters, welders, machine operators, etc.).

 

   EKOTRESTBUD constantly cooperates with universities in Lublin (Poland) and Lviv Polytechnic (Ukraine).

 

   ЕКОTRESTBUD has the experience of successful acquisition and development of foreign investment.

 

   The company's capabilities are confirmed by the rights, certificates, diplomas, licenses of the respective state inspection bodies, and therefore by the recommendations of our Clients and Partners.
